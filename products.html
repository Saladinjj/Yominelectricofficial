Single Phase Energy Meter

Manufactured in Wenzhou, China at our ISO9001 certified 18,000 m² facility, every unit undergoes rigorous calibration and quality testing before shipment. With low MOQ flexible ordering starting at 100 units, fast 15-25 day lead times, and comprehensive technical documentation, we enable utilities and distributors to deploy STS-compliant prepaid metering quickly and confidently in any market.

Current Transformer

As an established current transformer supplier China, Yomin Electric produces precision low voltage current transformers for metering, protection, and energy monitoring applications. Our CT range includes 5A and 1A secondary output models with primary current ratings from 50A to 3000A, designed to IEC 61869-2 and IEC 60044-1 standards. Manufactured at our ISO9001 certified production base in Wenzhou, China, every current transformer undergoes accuracy class testing (Class 0.2S, 0.5, 0.5S, 1) to ensure consistent linearity and phase displacement performance. We supply molded case, rectangular window, and busbar-type CTs suitable for panel mounting, LV switchgear, and retrofitted energy metering installations. OEM/ODM services are available with your branding, specific cable lengths, and custom terminal configurations, supported by low MOQ ordering for distributors and panel builders globally.

Split Core CT

Yomin Electric manufactures split core current transformers specifically designed for easy retrofitted installation without disconnecting existing conductors. Our split core CTs feature a hinged opening mechanism that clamps around cables or busbars, making them ideal for energy audits, sub-metering projects, and building management system integration. As a leading energy meter manufacturer China, we produce split core CTs with primary ratings from 5A to 5000A with 333mV, 1A, or 5A output options compatible with all major meter brands. Each unit is built under ISO9001 quality control at our Wenzhou, China factory and tested for accuracy to Class 0.5 or Class 1 standards. We offer OEM/ODM customization including custom cable lengths, connector types, and branded enclosures with low MOQ flexible ordering from 50 units, serving system integrators and energy management companies across 95+ countries worldwide.
