Nepal Container Loading: Primary & Secondary Current Transformers Shipped

LG
Logistics Team 3 min read June 23, 2026

Shipment Overview

We successfully loaded and dispatched multiple containers of primary and secondary current transformers to Nepal. This shipment represents a significant order that was manufactured at our ISO 9001:2015 certified facility in Wenzhou, Zhejiang, and packed for export with full quality documentation.

Each container was loaded under the supervision of our logistics and QC teams to ensure proper handling, secure packaging, and accurate inventory verification before dispatch.

Loading Process

1

Quality Inspection & Final Testing

Every unit underwent final QC inspection and testing before packing. Accuracy verification, insulation resistance, and visual inspection were completed per our standard export protocol.

2

Secure Packaging & Labeling

All CTs were packed in export-grade cartons with foam cushioning, moisture-resistant wrapping, and clear labeling showing product codes, quantities, and handling instructions.

3

Palletizing & Container Loading

Cartons were palletized on wooden pallets, stretch-wrapped for stability, and loaded into containers with proper weight distribution. Dock-level loading ensured safe handling.

4

Documentation & Customs Clearance

Commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, bill of lading, and test reports were prepared for Nepal customs clearance. All documentation aligned with export requirements.

5

Dispatch & Shipment Tracking

Containers departed from Ningbo/Zhejiang port with full shipment tracking enabled. Estimated transit time to Nepal is 15–20 days depending on the route.
