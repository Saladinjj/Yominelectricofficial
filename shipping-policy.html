Shipping Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Zhejiang Yomin Electric Co., Ltd. is a global exporter of precision electrical equipment. We manage complex logistics to ensure your order arrives safely and on time.

1. Free Global Shipping

We are pleased to offer **Free Global Shipping** on all standard B2B orders. This applies to bulk shipments and standard equipment orders placed through our official sales channels.

2. Ports of Departure

Our primary export hubs are located in China's leading ports to ensure maximum frequency and efficiency:

  • Port of Ningbo: Our primary port for sea freight.
  • Port of Shanghai: Used for specialized shipments and air freight.

3. Estimated Transit Times

While exact delivery dates depend on the destination country and customs processing, our average transit estimates are:

  • Sea Freight: 20–30 business days (standard for major global routes).
  • Air Freight/Express: 5–10 business days (available upon request for smaller components).

4. Order Handling & Packaging

Due to the sensitive nature of electrical metering and protection devices, all products are packed in reinforced, vibration-resistant export-grade crates. Handling time typically ranges from 3–7 business days depending on the volume and testing requirements of the order.

5. Customs & Duties

Unless specified in your formal quote (DDP terms), customers are responsible for local import duties and taxes in their respective countries. We provide all necessary documentation (Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Certificate of Origin) to facilitate smooth customs clearance.
