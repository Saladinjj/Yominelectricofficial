## How to Use

1. Open Seedance 2.0 in image-to-video mode
2. Drag all 8 files from `generative designs/seedance-product-images/` (logo + 7 products listed above, skip `04-voltage-stabilizer.jpg` and `06-tile-leveling-system.jpg`)
3. Paste the full text prompt above into the prompt field
4. Add the negative prompt
5. Settings: **16:9 / 25 seconds / 1080p or 2K**
6. Generate 2–3 versions, pick the cleanest result
7. Post-production: add voiceover, ambient score, and color grade in your editor
